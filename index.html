Instructions:

This page will display a picture-in-picture on your screen. So to start, follow these instructions:

  1. Go to YouTube and search for a video you want to play in the smaller window.
    I recommend "How to get a job after bootcamp." by CoderFoundry!
  2. Play that video in a separate window in full-screen mode.
  3. Come back to this window and refresh.
  4. A new modal window will load and select the "Application-window" section.
  5. Select the video from the windows that are open in your background.
  6. Press the "Share" button.
  7. Then on this page, press the start button.
  8. A new small video window will appear which can be resized.
  9. Enjoy watching your video while you work.

Technologies Used:

HTML5/CSS3/Javascript, using the Screen Capture API by Mozilla.
